About PookieFit 💕

Where fitness meets fun, and gains meet giggles

Our Story ✨

PookieFit was born from a simple idea: fitness should be fun, empowering, and inclusive. We believe you don't have to sacrifice personality for performance, or cute for capable.

Founded in 2024, we've built a community where everyone from complete beginners to fitness fanatics can feel at home. Our vibe? Think pink dumbbells, killer playlists, and the kind of supportive energy that makes you actually want to come to the gym.

What We Stand For 💪

❤️

Inclusivity First

Every body is a gym body. We celebrate all shapes, sizes, ages, and fitness levels. Your journey is yours, and we're here to support it.

🎯

Real Results

Cute vibes, serious gains. Our programs are designed by certified trainers who know how to help you reach your goals safely and effectively.

✨

Fun Over Everything

If you're not having fun, you won't stick with it. That's why we pack every class with energy, music, and good vibes.

🏆

Community Love

You're not just joining a gym—you're joining a family. Make friends, find workout buddies, and grow together.
